In the VEX Robotics Competition, the Skills Challenge allows teams to compete individually by testing both their driver control and autonomous programming abilities. Teams complete two types of runs—Driver Skills and Autonomous Coding Skills—to earn a combined score. These scores are ranked globally, making Skills one of the most important ways to measure a team's overall performance and consistency throughout the season.
